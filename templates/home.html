Frequently asked questions

How does ETSAI work for my buyers?
Your buyer clicks a link you send them (via Etsy messages, email, text — however you communicate). They see a friendly chat interface where an AI assistant asks them for the specs you need. It feels like texting a friend. No login required, no app to download. Takes under 2 minutes.
What if a buyer gives multiple answers in one message?
The AI handles it gracefully. If a buyer says "I want gold, size 7, with cursive font" — the AI extracts all three answers, confirms them, and moves on to whatever's still missing. It doesn't force buyers through a rigid checklist.
Can I customize the questions for each product?
Yes. When you add a product, the AI generates questions automatically — but you can edit, add, remove, or reorder them however you want. You can also set validation rules like character limits, required fields, and dropdown options.
Does it work with my existing Etsy workflow?
Perfectly. Connect your Etsy account to auto-import products and detect new orders. When someone buys, you just grab the intake link and paste it into Etsy messages. That's it. You can also import any listing by URL without connecting your account.
What happens when a buyer is confused or wants to cancel?
The AI detects frustration, confusion, and cancellation requests. It flags these conversations as "escalated" and sends you an email alert so you can step in personally. You always stay in control.
Can I try it before paying?
Absolutely. Every account starts with a 14-day free trial — full access, no credit card required. Set up takes about 5 minutes. If you're doing custom orders on Etsy, you'll see the value on your very first order.
